Our Mission

The Cube lowers the barriers to entrepreneurship at Duke by giving students the skills, resources, and community to build ventures of all kinds. Our alumni have launched multimillion-dollar companies and been featured by Forbes, CNN, and more.

What We Do

Support from first sketch to shipped product.

Who We Are

A community of computer scientists, engineers, economists, and business developers, providing comprehensive entrepreneurial support from evaluating product-market fit to technical development.

Programming

Public and private speaker series, entrepreneurship summits, and professional development workshops held throughout the year.

Partnerships

Close collaboration with Duke's entrepreneurship faculty, and active participation in the Duke Start-up Challenge and the Melissa & Doug Entrepreneurship Program.
